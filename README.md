### Unleash your coding powers with Shadowfax, a dark and sophisticated theme for VSCode

**Shadowfax** features a rich, dark palette with shades of indigo, navy blue, and charcoal grey, evoking the mystery and power of the night. Accents of silver and gold add a touch of elegance and sophistication, reminiscent of the intricate craftsmanship of the Elves

### Installation

1. Open Visual Studio Code.
2. Go to the Extensions view by clicking on the square icon on the left sidebar or pressing `Ctrl+Shift+X`.
3. Search for "Shadowfax" in the Extensions view.
4. Click on the "Install" button for the extension.
5. Once installed, you can activate the theme by clicking on the "Theme" dropdown in the top-right corner of the editor and selecting "Shadowfax".
